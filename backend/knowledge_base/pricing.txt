TechMart Electronics - Pricing & Plans

Basic Plan: Free. Includes standard product support and a 12-month warranty.

Premium Plan: $9.99/month. Includes 24-month extended warranty, priority
support with under-1-hour response time, free express shipping on all
orders, and early access to new product launches.

Business Plan: $29.99/month. Everything in Premium, plus bulk order
discounts (10%+ on orders of 10 or more units) and a dedicated account
manager.

Billing occurs monthly on the signup date. Plan changes take effect at the
start of the next billing cycle.
