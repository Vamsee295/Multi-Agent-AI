TechMart Electronics - Shipping Policy

Standard domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days and is free on orders over $50.
Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional $12.99.

Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days. You will receive a
tracking link by email once your order ships. Delayed shipments due to
weather or courier issues are eligible for shipping fee refunds upon request.
