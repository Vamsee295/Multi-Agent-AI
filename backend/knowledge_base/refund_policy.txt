TechMart Electronics - Refund Policy

Refunds are accepted within 30 days of purchase for unused items in original packaging.
Once a return is received and inspected, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
to the original payment method.

Digital subscriptions (e.g., Premium Plan) are refundable within 7 days of the charge
if the plan has not been used. After 7 days, subscriptions are non-refundable but can
be cancelled to prevent future renewal charges.

Defective products are eligible for a full refund or free replacement within the
12-month warranty period, regardless of the 30-day window above.
