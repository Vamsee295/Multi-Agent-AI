TechMart Electronics - Warranty Policy

All TechMart products come with a 12-month manufacturer warranty covering
defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty does not cover accidental
damage, water damage, or unauthorized repairs.

To file a warranty claim, contact support with your order number and a
description of the issue. Approved claims are resolved via free repair,
replacement, or refund, at TechMart's discretion.

Premium Plan subscribers receive an extended 24-month warranty automatically.
