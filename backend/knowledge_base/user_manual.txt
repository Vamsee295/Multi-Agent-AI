TechMart Electronics - User Manual (Account & Devices)

Account Setup
1. Visit techmart.example and click "Create Account".
2. Verify your email address before linking devices.
3. Sign in to the TechMart app (iOS/Android) or web dashboard.

Linking a Device
1. Open the TechMart app and tap "Add Device".
2. Put the device in pairing mode (hold the reset button for 5 seconds).
3. Select your Wi-Fi network and enter the password.
4. Wait for the green confirmation light — the device appears under "My Devices".

Login Troubleshooting
- If login fails, reset your password via "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page.
- Clear browser cache or update the TechMart app to the latest version.
- Two-factor authentication codes expire after 10 minutes; request a new code if needed.

Sync Issues
- Ensure the device has a stable Wi-Fi connection (2.4 GHz recommended).
- Force-sync from the app: Device Settings > Sync Now.
- If Premium features show as locked after payment, log out and back in to refresh entitlements.

Contact support@techmart.example with your account email and device model if issues persist.
